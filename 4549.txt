Types of Phishing
Q: Practice of sending fraudulent messages via text, email, or voice message pretending to be a reputable company or person in order to obtain personal information A: Phishing
Q: Practice of sending fraudulent messages via text pretending to be a reputable company or person in order to obtain personal information. A: Smishing
Q:Fraudulent practice of making phone calls or leaving voice messages pretending to be a reputable company or person in order to obtain personal information. A:Vishing
Q:Fraudulent practice of making phone calls, text, email, or voice messages pretending to be a reputable company or person in order to obtain personal information from a targeted individual. A:Spear Phishing
Q:Fraudulent practice of placing false QR codes in public pretending to be a reputable company or person in order to obtain personal information from an individual. A:Quishing
